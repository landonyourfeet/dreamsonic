What we do

Sessions combine consumer-grade EEG measurement with guided audio and light entrainment to help clients train their own relaxation, focus, and sleep-readiness responses. Each protocol targets a specific brainwave band — alpha for calm, theta for creativity, SMR for focus, and so on — paired with pre- and post-session self-reports to track change over time.

What this is not

DreamSonic is a wellness and performance practice. Sessions are not medical treatment, therapy, or counseling, and they are not a substitute for care by a licensed healthcare provider. If you're experiencing symptoms of a medical or psychological condition, please consult a qualified professional.

Who we serve

  • Adults pursuing relaxation, focus, creativity, or sleep training as personal goals
  • Clients who have completed a medical screening and signed informed consent
The BrainBit EEG headband used in sessions is a consumer / research-grade device, not FDA-cleared for clinical diagnosis. Photic stimulation carries a small risk for photosensitive individuals; all clients complete a medical screening before their first session. Participation is voluntary and may be stopped at any time.
